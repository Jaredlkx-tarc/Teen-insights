The meaning of idol to 21-year-old girl

By SOEW WEI SIN | Friday, 15 Aug 2025 |10:17pm MYT

KUALA LUMPUR: Nowadays, many youths are pursuing an idol or idol group, especially K-pop idol groups. K-pop is a type of music originating from South Korea.

Trace Yeo Tze Qing, 21-year-old girl who is pursuing a K-pop idol group, BOYNEXTDOOR. The idol group is formed by six boys.

She started to follow the idol group in 2023, which is the idol group debut. She will buy some merchandise related to them and attend their concert to support them.


The reason she likes them is because they are able to produce their own songs. From the lyrics to the rhythm, they took part to compose it. This has given her a feeling that they are talented and enthusiastic in their career.

She mentioned these vibes really hype her up and make her feel motivated as they are in the same age range with her. Every time she listens to their songs, she feels like she can achieve her dreams as well.
